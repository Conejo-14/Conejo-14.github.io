Description

My name is Anthony and I'm a graphic designer that by pure coincidence ended up in the wonderful world of decorated cookies and cakes. I started decorating only two years ago. I always liked baking but never really tried decorating, which is a completely different craft. It was Valentine's Day and I wanted to decorate some heart shaped cookies. I made some sugar glaze and watched some Youtube videos on cookie decorating. It took me a few hours to decorate just a few. I instantly fell in love with the process! Somehow, it felt pretty similar to drawing and painting. I continued reading and watching videos to learn as much as I could about all the tips and tricks to royal icing and sugar cookies; it’s still an ongoing process. I’m still learning with every set of cookies I decorate. The same thing with cakes.

As start?

Anthony’s Cookies & Cakes started as a way of sharing my love for both baking and decorating. I am not a professional baker, but I do enjoy the challenge of creating a piece of art from a small cookie.

How did you make the cakes?

I’m completely hands-on in the whole process. I make my own cutters or cut each cookie by hand, bake, decorate, seal and box every single cookie. I hand paint details and make from scratch all the elements of my cookies and cakes.

What inspires my designs?

Most of the cakes and cookies I’ve made have been inspired by pictures from other decorators. As I continue learning I hope to start adding my own designs and style more and more.

You dare?

If you feel adventurous enough to hire an amateur baker to make the sweets for your special occasion, feel free to reach out! And if you already have, please consider leaving a review. I do appreciate any feedback. It helps me improve.
